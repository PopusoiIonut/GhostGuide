# Privacy Policy for GhostGuide

**Last Updated:** April 2026

GhostGuide ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the GhostGuide mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect
GhostGuide is designed with privacy as the core fundamental property. **We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data on any external servers.**

### Location Data (CoreLocation)
The App uses Apple's native CoreLocation services to function. We request "Always" or "When In Use" location permissions to establish invisible geofences (tripwires) around historical monuments near you. 
*   **Local Processing Only:** Your GPS coordinates are processed entirely on your device. We do not transmit your location history, current location, or route data to any external server. 
*   **Geofencing:** Our API fetches historical landmarks based on your general proximity, and all trigger events happen locally on your iPhone.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services which may process data according to their own privacy policies:
*   **Wikipedia API:** The App briefly transmits your general geographic coordinates to the Wikimedia Foundation's public GeoSearch API to download the historical text of monuments near you. No personal identifiers are attached to this request.

## 3. Data Retention
Because we do not store your data on external servers, there is no data to retain or delete. If you wish to stop the App from accessing your location, you can revoke permissions at any time via your device's iOS Settings > Privacy > Location Services.

## 4. Audio Processing
Any audio playback or local speech synthesis runs entirely on your local iOS device using the `AVSpeechSynthesizer`. We do not record or process your microphone audio in any capacity.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the App Store contact link.
